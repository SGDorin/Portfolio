FEATURED GAME

Tectonic

Award-winning, Arcade Racer
Developed for: PlayStation® 4 & 5 and PlayStation®VR
On the Dreams™ platform

Read case study Play trailer

About the game

In Tectonic you Race through the city at high speed in 3 action-packed game modes, and unlock new parts to customize your car!
Watch the game in action here:

The game has been developed by a team of two people, My main roles as co-developer include:

Extra game
modes & content

In the game you start off by doing a normal race lap around the city, on lap 2 the city is hit by an earthquake and you race trough a distructable enviourment to reach safety. I've worked on extra content such as Bomb mode, where players race to secure checkpoints to extend the fuse on a bomb within their car

Optimising for
Virtual Reality

In VR you are inside the vehicle, fully immersed by the world while being in full control, and having he complete experience as on TV. I've made sure the sense of speed is kept while being mindful of the comfort optionsand help avoid making player motion sick.

UI Design and
Car Customization

By finishing the story or winning races and challanges, players unlock new customization parts to make their vehicle unique! I have designed the car 3D model and the customizable parts, and worked on all the game's UI elements

My Role on the project

On the 12th of July 2020, I've succesfully co-developed and released Tectonic, a racing game played by over 240k players on the Dreams Playstation platform, and held in high regards by players and in multiple game publications such as Eurogamer and ScreenRant.

My roles were to concept the main vehicle and create the 3D model, customisation parts, the UI & UX, Virtual Reality support and the Quality Assurance of the track collisions and car handling. On the 7th of August 2021 I have done the same and released an open world sequel called Frontier.
