Read case study Play trailer

About the game

In Tectonic you Race through the city at high speed in 3 action-packed game modes, and unlock new parts to customize your car!
Watch the game in action here:

The game has been developed by a team of two, Stefanescu Dorin & Sanders.
My main roles as co-developer include:

Extra game
modes & content

The game starts off with a lap around the city, however on lap 2 the city is hit by an earthquake and players race through a destructible environment, trying to reach the finish line safely. I've worked on extra content such as Bomb mode, where players race to secure checkpoints to extend the fuse on a bomb within their car, giving players another fun challenging mode.

Optimising for
Virtual Reality

I've added PlayStation VR compatibility, and began by adding a view from inside the vehicle. This has let players get fully immersed in the world, while still being in full control, and having the complete experience as on TV. I've made sure the drifting mechanic and sense of speed is kept. The vehicle's interior also acts as a visual anchor to help avoid making player motion sick while driving.

UI Design and
Car Customization

By finishing the story or winning specific challenges, players unlock new customization parts to make their vehicle unique! I have designed the car 3D model and the customizable parts and liveries, and worked on all the interfaces and menu in the game. The compatibility of the navigation and UI elements is kept consistent while wearing a PlayStation VR headset as well.

Project history

On the 12th of July 2020, I've successfully co-developed and released Tectonic, a racing game played by over 240k players on the Dreams PlayStation platform, and held in high regards by players and in multiple game publications such as Eurogamer and ScreenRant.

My roles were to create the marketing materials, concept the playable vehicle and create the 3D model, customization parts. As well as the UI & UX, Virtual Reality support and the Quality Assurance of the track collisions and car handling.
